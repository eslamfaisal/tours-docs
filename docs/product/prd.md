# Product Vision & Requirements Document

## Vision Statement

> Create the premier digital platform for discovering and booking authentic Egypt travel experiences, combining rich content with seamless booking and intelligent AI assistance.

---

## Product Goals

### Primary Goals

1. **Increase online bookings** — Convert website visitors into paying customers
2. **Enhance discoverability** — Improve SEO and content marketing
3. **Streamline operations** — Automate booking and customer management
4. **Expand market reach** — Support multilingual audiences

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Conversion Rate | > 3% |
| Booking Completion | > 70% |
| Page Load Time | < 2s |
| Customer Satisfaction | > 4.5/5 |
| Organic Traffic Growth | +50% YoY |

---

## Core Features

### 1. Trip Discovery

- Browse tours by type (packages, day tours, cruises)
- Filter by destination, duration, price, rating
- Rich trip pages with itinerary, inclusions, gallery
- Related trips and recommendations

### 2. Online Booking

- **Instant Booking** — immediate confirmation
- **Request-to-Book** — availability confirmation required
- Flexible payment (deposit or full)
- Real-time availability calendar

### 3. Customer Portal

- Account management
- Booking history and status
- Invoices and payment records
- Support ticket system
- Wishlist/favorites

### 4. Content Hub

- Blog articles (travel tips, destination guides)
- Vlogs (video content)
- Past trip galleries
- Customer reviews and testimonials

### 5. AI Assistant

- Natural language trip search
- Personalized recommendations
- Booking assistance
- FAQ answering

### 6. Admin Backoffice

- Trip and destination management
- Booking operations
- Customer management
- Content management (blog/vlog)
- Analytics dashboard
- Translation workflow

## Scope Constraints

| In Scope | Out of Scope |
|----------|--------------|
| Egypt destinations only | International tours |
| Web platform | Native mobile apps (Phase 1) |
| Online payments | Cash on arrival |
| Email/Chat support | Phone support system |
